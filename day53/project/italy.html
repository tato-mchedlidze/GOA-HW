Grand Hotel Tremezzo

A little review of our Luxurious hotel:


Grand Hotel Tremezzo is a luxury hotel on the shores of Lake Como in Tremezzo, Italy, known for its historic charm and stunning views. Opened in 1910, it blends Belle Époque architecture with modern luxury. Guests enjoy elegant rooms, a floating pool on the lake, a spa, fine dining, and activities like boat trips and hiking. It’s a top destination for those seeking relaxation and glamour, attracting celebrities and royals over the years.

You will encounter breathtaking views!!!

Lake View Prestige Room

Walk in, and let your eye be inevitably drawn to the French doors that lead onto your balcony, where there's a view stretching from Bellagio to Balbianello.
It might be hard to ever come back inside; but when you do, the soaring ceilings, delicate palette, and beautifully-framed period prints and paintings will provoke their own delight.

Terrace Garden Suite

These special suite have a separate sitting room, an indulgent bathroom (with a second, smaller one too), and a wide terrace bathed in sunlight and boasting views across the lake to Bellagio and up to our Belvedere.
The perfect place for a family, with room to play—and enjoy an alfresco aperitivo.
